🏓 KitchenSync Matchmaking Dashboard

🔄 System Logic & Features Overview:
  • 👤 Player Profiles: Click any player's name in the leaderboard to open their dynamic, individual performance sheet.
  • 🤖 Auto-Matchmaker: Automatically generates matches based on priority (fewest games played & longest wait time). Intelligently mixes Solos and Fixed Duos.
  • 🎱 Dynamic Pools: Players start in the ⚪ Neutral pool. Match winners advance to the 🏆 Winners pool, and losers drop to the 📉 Losers pool. Matchups stay segmented within these tiers.
  • 👥 Fixed Duos: Lock pairs together. They queue as a single unit, and pausing/resuming one instantly syncs their partner's state.
  • ⏸️ Status Management: Players can be toggled to 🔴 Resting to skip matchmaking. Players on courts or queued must have their matches canceled before removal or resting.
  • ⛓️ Multi-Sort Leaderboard: Check the Multi-Sort toggle (or hold Shift while clicking headers) to chain multiple sorting criteria (e.g., sort by Wins, then Point Diff).
  • 📝 Data Corrections: Tap the ✏️ edit icon in the roster below to update names without losing stats. Use Bulk Add for fast setups.
  • 🔄 Flexible Resets: Clear upcoming matches, reset pools to Neutral, perform a Soft Reset (keeps roster, wipes stats), or Hard Reset (wipes everything).
