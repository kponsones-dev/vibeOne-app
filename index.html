🏓 KitchenSync - Matchmaking System (VibeCode v6.4.2026.930)

✨ KitchenSync Features:
• Asynchronous Courts: Manage matches independently. Start or finish games on individual courts without waiting for others.
• Smart Matchmaking: The auto-pairing engine prioritizes players with the fewest games and longest wait times, while actively avoiding immediate rematches.
• Fixed Teams: Lock specific pairs of players together to guarantee they play as a duo.
• Rest & Pause Mode: Temporarily sit players out of the matchmaking pool without losing their core data.
• Live Analytics: Real-time leaderboard tracking Wins, Losses, Points For (PF), Points Against (PA), and Point Differential (PD).
• ⚠️ Permanent Late Penalty: Flagging a player as late instantly resets them to the absolute back of the rotation and permanently marks their record with a late badge for the session.
