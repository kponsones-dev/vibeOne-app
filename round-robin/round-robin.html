🏓 KitchenSync (Round Robin)

✨ System Features:
• Asynchronous Courts: Manage matches independently.
• Smart Matchmaking: Prioritizes longest wait times & fewest games.
• Fixed Teams: Lock specific pairs of players together.
  • Active Courts: You cannot link players if either is currently playing on a court.
  • Waiting Queue: If players are waiting in the queue, linking them will instantly lock them together for the next available court!
• Rest Mode: Pause players without losing their stats.
• ⚠️ Late Penalty: Force late players to the back of the queue.
• 📊 Multi-Column Sort: Turn on "Multi-Sort Mode" below or hold Shift on desktop to chain columns.
